BangaluruRides – Cab Rental Web App

June 2024 - Apr 2025

Role & Responsibilities

As the lead full-stack developer, I took on the personal project of building a complete cab rental web application for a family business. My goal was to digitize and streamline their operations, from booking to dispatch and payment. I was responsible for the entire technical execution, turning a traditional business model into a modern, web-based service.

This project involved close collaboration with the business stakeholders to understand their needs and translate them into technical requirements. I designed and implemented a user-friendly interface for customers and a robust backend for managing operations.

Key Achievements & Technologies

  • Full-Stack Development: Built a complete, production-ready application from scratch using the MERN stack.
  • Business Process Automation: Designed and implemented a dynamic fare calculation engine and an order management system to streamline booking, cancellation, and tracking.
  • Stakeholder Collaboration: Successfully supported my father’s and his associates’ cab services, leading to easier customer acquisition and scheduling.
  • Core Technologies: React.js, Node.js, Express.js, Firebase (Firestore, Auth), Vercel.
