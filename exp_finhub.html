FinHub - Stock Market Dashboard

June 2024 - Apr 2025

Role & Responsibilities

As the lead developer of FinHub, I was responsible for creating a React-based dashboard designed to act as a financial command center for investors. My primary role was to architect the front-end and manage the integration of various financial APIs to deliver live, actionable market data.

I designed and implemented features for portfolio tracking, stock trend analysis, and interactive charting. This project was paused due to the competitive market landscape and lack of a team, but it stands as a significant demonstration of my ability to handle complex data visualization and API integration challenges.

Key Achievements & Technologies

  • Complex UI/UX Design: Developed a sophisticated and intuitive dashboard for displaying dense financial information, including a market heatmap and economic calendar.
  • API Integration: Successfully integrated multiple real-time financial APIs to create a single, cohesive data source for users.
  • Data Visualization: Implemented interactive charts and graphs to help users analyze market trends and track their portfolios effectively.
  • Core Technologies: React.js, Chart.js (or similar), Financial Data APIs.
