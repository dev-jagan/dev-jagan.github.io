About Me

I am a product-driven engineer, and my story is one of prioritizing practical application over pure theory. While my formal academic path provided a foundation, my true education began when I dove headfirst into building real-world applications. My focus has always been on the end product, which led me to master the entire ecosystem of development tools—from front-end to back-end—long before I was concerned with academic grades or attendance records.

This hands-on obsession is best exemplified by one of my earliest major projects, 'VTU StudyUp'. This application, which took a dedicated year and a half of self-directed learning to build, became the cornerstone of my practical education. It was a testament to my belief in learning by doing. The recent explosion in AI has supercharged this lifelong habit of intense self-learning, accelerating my development process by an order of magnitude. This allows me to rapidly develop sophisticated applications for clients and my own ventures, including a suite of successful apps on the Google Play Store and commercial platforms like beriqa.com.

This journey—from a builder focused on tools, to a persistent self-taught developer, to an AI-augmented engineer—has given me a unique and pragmatic perspective. I don't just write code; I build successful, market-ready products.
