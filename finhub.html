FinHub - Stock Market Dashboard

About the Project

FinHub is a sophisticated financial command center I developed to provide investors with a comprehensive, real-time overview of the market. The dashboard integrates multiple financial APIs to deliver live market data, interactive charts, and up-to-the-minute news feeds.

The platform was designed to support informed investment decisions by offering advanced features like portfolio tracking, stock trend analysis, a market heatmap, and an economic calendar. This project showcases my ability to work with complex data streams and present them in a clean, intuitive user interface.

My Role & Technologies

I was the lead developer for this project. My responsibilities included designing the UI/UX, integrating the financial APIs, and building the interactive components.

  • Frontend: React.js
  • Data Sources: Financial APIs for market data, news, and analysis.
  • Key Features: Real-time market overview, financial news feed, market heatmap (S&P 500, NASDAQ, Crypto), customizable watchlist, and technical analysis tools.
